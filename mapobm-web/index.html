About Us

Since 2009, Mandalawangi Outbound has been at the forefront of delivering exceptional outdoor experiences in Kabupaten Cianjur. We specialize in organizing comprehensive event programs that combine adventure, team building, and fun. Whether you’re looking for corporate training, adventure games, or a serene camping experience, our well-crafted activities are designed to strengthen bonds, challenge limits, and create lasting memories.

At Mandalawangi Outbound, we pride ourselves on creating tailor-made programs that cater to diverse groups and objectives. Our team of experienced facilitators and guides ensures that every activity is both safe and enjoyable, offering you the best in outdoor event management.
